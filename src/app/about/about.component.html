ABOUT OUR ELEMENTARY SCHOOL

Alphonsa School, Kalamjote, a unit of the Koderma Franciscan Clarist Sister's Society is a Christian Minority School under the management of the Franciscan Clarist Sisters who are engaged in Educational, Medical and Social work all over India Since its origin on 14th December 1888. Alphonsa School was established on 3rd April 1995 and since then one grade was added every year until it became a full-fledged High School having I.C.S.E syllabus. It is a co-educational school
